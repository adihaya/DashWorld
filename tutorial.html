Welcome to the Official Tutorial and FAQ for DashWorld!
What is DashWorld?
Well, DashWorld is a dashboard. Dashboards are places, where you can see information relavant to all times and places. Dashboards are where you can quickly access information you might need, like the time, date, your current location, your next meeting, etc. DashWorld is a dashboard, like that. But it's completely built in HTML and JavaScript, 2 powerful languages. It's very sophisticated, too.
How do I access DashWorld?
DashWorld, is a Chrome app. You can go to the Chrome Apps page, to see all your chrome apps. From there, click the blue rocket icon, to launch DashWorld. If you have the Chrome Apps Launcher, you can also Launch it from there.
I have feedback. Where do I submit?
Glas you asked! Fork the DashWorld git repository, and make changes, if you are a developer. We will see the changes, and might submit them in our app, and credit you. If you're not a developer, send an email to tt2d[at]icloud[dot]com with your feedback/ideas. Thanks!
